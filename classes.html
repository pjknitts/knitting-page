Classes

  • Learn How To Knit

    Come learn how to knit on Friday March 4th from 7-9 cost of the class is $25, which includes materials. You will learn how to cast-on, the knit stitch, purl stitch and binding-off.

  • Learn How To Crochet

    Come learn how to crochet on Wednesday March 15th from 5-7 cost of class is $25, which includes materials. You will learn how to chain, single crochet, double crochet, and half double crochet.

  • Learn How To Spin

    Come learn how to spin fiber into yarn on a spinning wheel on Monday March 12th from 6-8 cost of the class is $45, which includes materials and use of spinning wheel. You will learn how to treadle a spinning wheel, drafting your fiber, and how to ply your fiber.

  • Learn How To Knit Lace

    Come learn how to knit lace on Saturday March 21st from 12-3 cost of class is $25, which includes materials. You will learn how to read your lace, how to make a yarn over, and a few different type of decreases.

  • Knitting 911

    Come and get help on your project. Every Tuesday from 4-9 cost of the class is $10. You will get one on one time with someone that can help you with a mistake you may have made, help reading your pattern, and any general help you may need.
